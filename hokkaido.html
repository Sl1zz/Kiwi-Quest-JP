Hokkaido

Hotel Recommendations for Hokkaido

Luxury Hotels

  • Kyukaruizawa Kikyo, Hokkaido: Located in the luxurious resort area of Kyukaruizawa, this upscale hotel offers elegant rooms, hot springs, and stunning natural surroundings.
  • Sapporo Grand Hotel: Situated in the heart of Sapporo, this luxurious hotel boasts classic charm, fine dining, and easy access to the city's attractions.
  • The Windsor Hotel Toya: Found in Lake Toya, this opulent hotel provides breathtaking lake views, spacious accommodations, and onsen facilities.

Mid-Range Hotels

  • Hotel MyStays Sapporo Aspen: Located in the Susukino entertainment district of Sapporo, this modern hotel offers comfortable rooms and is within walking distance of restaurants and shopping.
  • Asahikawa Toyo Hotel: Situated in Asahikawa, this hotel provides cozy rooms, a convenient location, and easy access to Asahiyama Zoo and Furano's lavender fields.
  • Furano Natulux Hotel: Found in the town of Furano, this mid-range hotel offers comfortable accommodations, hot spring baths, and easy access to the town's attractions.

Budget-Friendly Options

  • Stay Sapporo Hostel: A budget-friendly choice in Sapporo, offering dormitory-style rooms, private rooms, and a friendly atmosphere for travelers.
  • Guesthouse Nari-Nari: Located in Otaru, this guesthouse provides affordable rooms, a relaxed environment, and proximity to Otaru's charming canal area.
  • Lavender Lodge Furano: Found in Furano, this lodge offers budget-friendly accommodations, making it a great choice for skiers and nature enthusiasts.

Unique Stays

  • Niseko Tabi-Tsumugi Backpackers Hostel: A unique hostel in Niseko, offering dormitory beds, a communal lounge, and easy access to Niseko's world-class ski slopes.
  • Yunokawa Onsen Green Hotel: Located in Hakodate, this hotel provides a unique onsen experience with open-air baths overlooking the sea.
  • Farm Tomita Cottage: A unique stay in Furano, offering cottage accommodations amid the beautiful lavender and flower fields of Farm Tomita.
