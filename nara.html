Nara

Hotel Recommendations for Nara

Luxury Hotels

  • Nara Hotel: Located in Nara Park, this luxurious hotel offers elegant rooms, historic charm, and a serene atmosphere amidst the deer of Nara.
  • Hotel Nikko Nara: Situated near Nara Park, this upscale hotel provides comfortable rooms, a spa, and easy access to the city's historic attractions.
  • Noborioji Hotel Nara: Found in the Naramachi area, this opulent hotel offers a combination of traditional Japanese aesthetics and modern amenities.

Mid-Range Hotels

  • Sun Hotel Nara: Located in the city center, this modern hotel offers comfortable rooms and easy access to Nara's temples and museums.
  • Nara Kenko Land Plaza Hotel: Situated near Nara Koen Park, this hotel provides a range of amenities, including a hot spring bath and relaxation facilities.
  • Oyado Nihon no Ashitaba: A traditional ryokan in Nara, offering a unique and intimate Japanese experience with tatami rooms and kaiseki cuisine.

Budget-Friendly Options

  • Nara Guesthouse Kamunabi: A budget-friendly choice in the Naramachi area, offering dormitory-style rooms, private rooms, and a friendly atmosphere.
  • Guesthouse Nara Komachi: Located in the Naramachi district, this guesthouse features modern amenities and a welcoming environment for travelers.
  • Nara Ugaya Guesthouse: Found in the city center, this guesthouse offers both dormitory and private rooms, making it suitable for a range of budgets.

Unique Stays

  • Nara Deer Hostel: A unique hostel located near Nara Park, offering a memorable experience with Nara's famous deer as your neighbors.
  • Nara Ugaya Inn: A charming guesthouse in the traditional Naramachi area, providing a unique glimpse into Nara's history and culture.
  • Guesthouse Tsunoya: A traditional machiya-style guesthouse in Nara, featuring classic Japanese architecture and a cozy atmosphere.
