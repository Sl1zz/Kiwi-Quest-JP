Tokyo

Hotel Recommendations

Luxury Hotels

  • Aman Tokyo: Located in the Otemachi financial district, this is a luxurious hotel with stunning city views, a serene spa, and exceptional service.
  • The Ritz-Carlton Tokyo: Situated in Roppongi, this upscale hotel offers elegant rooms, Michelin-starred dining, and a panoramic city view from its spa.
  • The Peninsula Tokyo: Overlooking Hibiya Park, this opulent hotel offers spacious rooms, top-notch restaurants, and a beautiful rooftop bar.

Mid-Range Hotels

  • Shibuya Excel Hotel Tokyu: Located in the heart of Shibuya, this hotel provides comfortable rooms and great access to shopping, dining, and transportation.
  • Hotel Gracery Shinjuku: Situated in Shinjuku, this modern hotel offers reasonably priced rooms, a convenient location, and even a Godzilla-themed room with a view of the famous monster.
  • Ginza Creston Hotel: Found in the upscale Ginza district, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and easy access to high-end shopping and dining.

Budget-Friendly Options

  • Khaosan World Asakusa Ryokan & Hostel: A budget-friendly choice in Asakusa, offering dormitory-style rooms, private rooms, and a friendly atmosphere.
  • Mustard Hotel Asakusa 2: Located in Asakusa, this hostel provides affordable private and dormitory rooms and is within walking distance of Senso-ji Temple.
  • Nui. Hostel & Bar Lounge: A stylish hostel in Asakusa with a lively bar and communal areas, offering both private and dormitory-style rooms.

Unique Stays

  • Henn na Hotel Tokyo Asakusabashi: This hotel in Asakusabashi features robotic staff and a futuristic theme, providing a unique experience.
  • Book and Bed Tokyo: Located in Ikebukuro, this hostel has bookshelves built into the bunk beds, making it a haven for book lovers.
  • The Millennials Shibuya: A capsule hotel in Shibuya designed with modern travelers in mind, offering compact yet comfortable sleeping pods.
