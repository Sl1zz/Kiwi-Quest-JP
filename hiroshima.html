Hiroshima

Hotel Recommendations for Hiroshima

Luxury Hotels

  • Sheraton Grand Hiroshima Hotel: Located in the city center, this luxurious hotel offers elegant rooms, fine dining, and easy access to Hiroshima's attractions.
  • Hiroshima Grand Prince Hotel: Situated along the shores of the Seto Inland Sea, this upscale hotel provides serene views, modern amenities, and beautiful gardens.
  • ANA Crowne Plaza Hiroshima: Found in the Peace Memorial Park area, this opulent hotel offers comfortable accommodations and is within walking distance of Peace Memorial Park and the Atomic Bomb Dome.

Mid-Range Hotels

  • Capsule Hotel Cube Hiroshima: Located in the city center, this modern capsule hotel offers compact yet comfortable capsules and a unique accommodation experience.
  • Hotel Granvia Hiroshima: Situated above Hiroshima Station, this hotel provides convenient access to transportation and comfortable rooms with city views.
  • RIHGA Royal Hotel Hiroshima: Found in the city center, this mid-range hotel offers a variety of amenities, including a pool and a range of dining options.

Budget-Friendly Options

  • J-Hoppers Hiroshima Guesthouse: A budget-friendly choice in the city center, offering dormitory-style rooms, private rooms, and a friendly atmosphere for travelers.
  • Backpackers Hostel K's House Hiroshima: Located near Hiroshima Station, this hostel provides affordable accommodations and is a great base for exploring the city and nearby attractions.
  • Sakura Guest House: Found in the center of Hiroshima, this guesthouse offers budget-friendly private and dormitory rooms in a welcoming environment.

Unique Stays

  • Hiroshima Capsule Hotel & Sauna: A unique capsule hotel experience with sauna facilities, located in the city center of Hiroshima.
  • Hiroshima Pacific Hotel: A historic hotel in the city center with a retro charm and a unique blend of modern and vintage aesthetics.
  • Miyajima Guest House Mikuniya: Located on Miyajima Island, this guesthouse offers a unique stay with traditional Japanese accommodation and friendly hosts.
